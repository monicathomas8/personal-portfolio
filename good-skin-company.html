The Good Skin Company

The Good Skin Company is a professional website for a skin clinic offering a range of treatments and services, alongside an online retail store for skincare products. The client requested a platform they could easily manage themselves after handover, so the site was built on Squarespace, demonstrating my ability to choose the right tool for the client's needs rather than a one-size-fits-all approach. Key features include:

  • Custom design and layout to reflect the brand's identity and values.
  • Integration of an e-commerce platform for seamless online shopping experience.
  • Mobile-responsive design to ensure accessibility across all devices.
  • Easy-to-use content management system for the client to update products, services, and content independently.
  • SEO optimization to enhance online visibility and attract potential customers.
  • Custom HTML and CSS blocks for bespoke styling beyond Squarespace defaults.
