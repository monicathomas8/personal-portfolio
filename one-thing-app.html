One Thing App

One Thing is a calm, mobile-first Progressive Web App designed to help reduce overwhelm by guiding you to focus on just one meaningful task at a time. Instead of long to-do lists and pressure to do everything, One Thing encourages you to:

  • Identify the one most important task you want to accomplish.
  • Get your thoughts out of your head.
  • Tune into the vibe of each task.
  • Choose one gentle focus for today.
  • Breathe before you begin.
Built as a PWA, it can be installed on desktop and mobile and works just like a native app - no app store needed. Give me the vibe, I'll do the sorting. You're not doing this alone. This started as a one-day practice project and has grown into an ongoing open source app, updated with new features as real user requests come in.
